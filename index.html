About

Banoyo Integrated National High School is a public educational institution committed to providing quality and inclusive education for learners in the community. The school aims to develop knowledgeable, disciplined, and responsible students by promoting academic excellence, values formation, and holistic growth. Through dedicated teachers and supportive programs, the school continues to nurture future-ready individuals.

History

Banoyo Integrated National High Banoyo National High School, located in Barangay Banoyo, San Luis, Batangas, was established through legislative action during the 12th Congress of the Philippines . The school was created to provide accessible secondary education for the locality, with its establishment authorized by House Bill No. 3044, which included funding for its operation.
